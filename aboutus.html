About Me

Colonel Mark McCauley (Ret, USAF) is the owner & operator of Fighter Pilot Home Inspection & Repair. His military career spans over 27 years as a fighter pilot in the F-22 & F-16, and weapons system officer in the F-4. He was the former Wing Commander of the Virginia Air National Guard at Langley AFB. He received his BS in Aerospace & Ocean Engineering from Virginia Tech and his MS in Human Resource Management from Troy State University. He is an FAA certified Airline Transport Pilot, an American Home Inspector Training Institute graduate, a Virginia Class-C contractor, a Virginia Certified Inspector, a Certified Residential Thermographer, skilled woodworker, and has over 30 years experience in home repairs & improvements.
