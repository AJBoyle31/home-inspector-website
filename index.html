Home Inspections

A thorough home inspection of all systems & components, hidden areas such as the foundation & attic, plus a complete interior & exterior examination is crucial before you close the deal. Fighter Pilot Home Inspection & Repair will systematically check the "whole house" with scrutiny that exceeds the American Society of Home Inspectors Standards of Practice. You'll be provided with a comprehensive written report that will give you the ability to negotiate the purchase with greater assurance. We'll even walk you through the residence and point out areas of major concern, items not operating, potential safey hazards, and future deferred cost items. Rest assured, you will have complete peace of mind before you buy!
